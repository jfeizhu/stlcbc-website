歷任牧者 / Pastoral History

1979-1995

游天度牧師 / Pastor Joseph Yu

Pastor Joseph Yu was called to serve as the first pastor of STLCBC after the church was officially incorporated on May 6, 1979. He faithfully shepherded the congregation until his retirement in 1995.

1996+

黃應傑牧師 / Pastor Ying-Kit Wong

In December 1996, Pastor Ying-Kit Wong became the church’s second full-time pastor. His ministry continued during a key season of church growth, expansion, and relocation.

牧者的心志 / The Heart of Pastoral Ministry

Chinese

牧者的職分不只是講台上的教導，也包括在日常生活中陪伴會眾， 用聖經真理勉勵人、在軟弱時扶持人、在禱告中記念人，並帶領教會同心事奉主。

我們盼望這一頁成為初次認識教會的朋友的一個窗口，看見神如何藉著忠心的牧者持續建立祂的教會。

English

Pastoral ministry is more than preaching on Sundays. It also means walking with people in everyday life, encouraging them with Scripture, strengthening them in hardship, and leading the church to serve Christ together.

This page offers a simple introduction to the pastoral heart of STLCBC and the faithful leadership God has provided over the years.
