信仰告白
Faith Statement

These statements summarize the core beliefs of St. Louis Chinese Baptist Church in both Chinese and English.

我們相信：

聖經是神的完備的無誤的話語，是教會和基督徒信仰生活的唯一準則（提後3：16-17）。

耶穌基督是神成為肉身。他為我們的罪死，被埋葬，第三天從死裏復活，升天，必定再來審判活人死人（提前3：16）。

聖靈進入到認罪悔改人的心裏，引導信徒明白聖經，以榮耀神為生活目標（約16：8-14）。

聖父吸引人信靠他的兒子耶穌基督。神是聖潔公義的，也是慈愛憐憫的（出34：6-7）。

神造男人和女人（創1：26-27）。人由於不榮耀神，也不感謝神,人的心完全敗壞，充滿詭詐。我們無力自救。只有相信耶穌基督，罪人才能逃避將來神的憤怒審判（弗3：14-21）。

因信基督徒稱義，成聖，並且將來與主同在榮耀裏（羅8：30）。

在悖逆的世界中，聖靈借著神的話必定能保守基督徒戰勝所有嚴峻考驗，並把福音廣傳（弗3：14-21）。

神設立地方教會成為信徒的家，是真理的柱石（提前3：15）。

上帝所設立的婚姻是一個盟約，是能生養的兩性關系，是一男一女、一夫一妻、一生一世的聯合，也預表基督和祂的新婦——教會，之間的盟約之愛（弗5：22-33）。

We believe:

The Bible is the inerrant and sufficient word of God, the only standard of church and Christian life (2Tim 3:16).

Jesus Christ is God in flesh. He died for our sin, resurrected on the third day, was buried and ascended. He will come a second time to judge the living and the dead (1Tim 3:16).

The Holy Spirit abides in the repentant heart, and leads believers to understand the Bible, and helps them live a life glorifying God (John 16:8-14).

The Holy Father draws people to believe in Jesus Christ. God is holy and just, he is also loving and merciful (Exo 34:6-7).

God created man and woman (Gen 1:26-27). Because humans do not glorify God nor thank him, man’s heart is completely corrupt and crafty. We cannot save ourselves. Only through believing Jesus Christ can we escape the angry judgment of God (Rom 3:9-25).

By faith in Jesus a Christian goes through justification, sanctification and glorification (Rom 8:30).

In the rebellious world, the Holy Spirit can help Christians overcome any ordeal through God’s word, and help them preach the gospel (Eph 3:14-21).

God ordains the local church to be the family of believers, and the pillar of truth (1Tim 3:15).

God ordains marriage to be a covenant between a man and a woman, a husband and a wife. Marriage illustrates how Christ loves his bride—the church (Eph 5:22-33).
