Children Sunday School

Leading children to a saving knowledge of the Lord Jesus Christ and helping them grow in faithful, Spirit-led lives.

Our Purpose

異象與使命 / Vision And Mission

中文

帶領孩子們對我們的主耶穌基督有一個得救的知識,並激勵他們在聖靈的感動下過上忠心和聖潔的生活。

English

Leading the children to a saving knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ and inspiring them to live faithful and honorable lives by the help of the Holy Spirit.

Weekly Schedule

聚會安排 / Sunday Schedule

1st Session

第一節 / 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

課程 / Curriculum: 小羊羔中文班 / The Little Lamb

  • 提供專為主日學兒童設計的簡體中文課程。
  • 加深對聖經的認識。
  • 提升中文的聽、說、讀、寫能力。
  • 在充滿喜樂、以信仰為中心的環境中快樂學習。
  • Offers Simplified Chinese courses designed specifically for Sunday School children.
  • Deepens their understanding of the Bible.
  • Develops Chinese listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.
  • Encourages learning in a joyful, faith-centered environment.
2nd Session

第二節 / 11:15 AM - 12:15 PM

  • 點心交流時間 - 為孩子們提供一個分享點心、彼此交流、學習餐桌禮儀的溫馨空間。
  • 聖經故事時間 - 透過有意義的聖經故事和互動式問答，引導孩子們認識上帝並加深對祂作為的理解。
  • Snack & Chat - a space for children to share, connect, and learn table manners in a relaxed, social setting.
  • Bible Stories - helping children know God through meaningful storytelling and interactive questions that deepen understanding.
